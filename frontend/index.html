About This Project

How our AI-powered resume screening works

Our Resume Screening application uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to match candidate resumes with job descriptions. By analyzing the semantic content of both documents, we can provide accurate similarity scores and rank candidates based on their fit for the position.

The system processes resumes by extracting text content, cleaning and normalizing the text, and then comparing it against the job description using TF-IDF vectorization and cosine similarity algorithms. This approach goes beyond simple keyword matching to understand the contextual relevance of each candidate.
